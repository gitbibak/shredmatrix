## LinkedIn Post

I built Full Balance as a mobile-first wellness dashboard for the daily loop: plan, train, eat, track, reflect and repeat.

The current product combines onboarding, workout planning, nutrition and macro targets, calorie lookup, water and sleep tracking, progress measurements, achievements, profile photos, data export and share cards.

The strongest product decision is that Full Balance is free forever. No subscription layer, no card-first onboarding, no premium wall.

The main design goal was density without chaos: everything should be close enough to use daily, but calm enough that users do not feel punished by the interface.

Live at fullbalance.app.
