# Campaign Asset Map

All campaign visuals are available in Turkish and English with matching compositions.

Filename pattern:
`assets/campaigns/campaign-{angle}-{ratio}-{language}.png`

Languages:

- `tr` — Turkish
- `en` — English

Ratios:

- `wide-16x9` — `1920x1080`
- `square-1x1` — `1080x1080`
- `portrait-4x5` — `1080x1350`
- `story-9x16` — `1080x1920`

## Layout Note

The latest campaign templates keep CTA text inside the main copy block instead of anchoring it to the bottom edge. Product screenshots are intentionally pushed lower in `1:1` and `4:5` formats so long Turkish headlines do not sit underneath the visuals.
