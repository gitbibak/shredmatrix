# Full Balance Campaign Copy Playbook

This file maps the exported campaign visuals to ready-to-use launch and evergreen copy.

Core promise:
`Tamamen ücretsiz. Her zaman ücretsiz. Asla ücretli olmayacak.`

English promise:
`Completely free. Always free. No subscription, no card, no paywall.`

Do not use paid-tier language: `trial`, `upgrade`, `Plus`, `Pro`, `premium`, `subscribe`, `unlock`, `limited free plan`.

## Format Guide

Use the same filename pattern for every campaign:
`assets/campaigns/campaign-{angle}-{ratio}-{language}.png`

Ratios:

- `wide-16x9` — Product Hunt updates, LinkedIn link posts, X image posts, blog/landing sections, YouTube/short demo cover.
- `square-1x1` — Instagram feed, X, LinkedIn feed, Gumroad update image, community posts.
- `portrait-4x5` — Instagram feed ads, Facebook/Meta feed, LinkedIn promoted image, mobile-first announcement.
- `story-9x16` — Instagram Stories, Reels cover, TikTok cover, YouTube Shorts cover, WhatsApp/Telegram story share.

Recommended funnel:

- Awareness: `free-forever`, `beginner-start`, `no-app-switching`, `multilingual-pwa`
- Consideration: `daily-loop`, `personal-plan`, `nutrition-habits`, `workout-phase`, `recovery-signals`
- Retention/proof: `progress-proof`, `weekly-reflection`, `share-progress`, `data-export`, `mobile-data`

CTA pool:

- Turkish: `Ücretsiz başla`, `fullbalance.app'i aç`, `Planını ücretsiz oluştur`, `Bugünün dengesini kur`
- English: `Start free`, `Open fullbalance.app`, `Build your free plan`, `Find today’s balance`
