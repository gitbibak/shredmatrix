# Full Balance Positioning

## Core Position

Full Balance is a free-forever, mobile-first personal wellness dashboard that brings training, nutrition, water, sleep, progress, achievements and sharing into one daily routine.

The most important marketing promise:
`Tamamen ĂĽcretsiz. Her zaman ĂĽcretsiz. Asla ĂĽcretli olmayacak.`

## Short Positioning Options

1. Free forever wellness planning for body, mind and nutrition.
2. One free daily dashboard for workouts, meals and progress.
3. A mobile-first fitness and nutrition plan with no paywall.
4. Your free forever plan for training, meals, habits and progress.
5. A calmer way to follow your wellness routine, always free.

Recommended:
`Free forever wellness planning for body, mind and nutrition.`

## Primary Audience

- People who want structure without hiring a personal trainer immediately.
- Beginners and intermediate users who need a clear daily plan.
- People tracking both workout and nutrition habits.
- Mobile-first users who want a free, fast, web-based fitness companion.
- Users who care about consistency signals: water, sleep, measurements, streaks and badges.
- Users tired of trial countdowns, premium walls, subscription prompts and card-first onboarding.

## Messaging Pillars

### Free Forever

Full Balance is not a freemium funnel. The core promise is permanent free access.

Use:
`No subscription. No card. No premium wall. Full Balance stays free.`

Avoid:
`Free trial`, `upgrade`, `Plus`, `premium`, `paid tier`, `limited free plan`.

### Personal Plan

Onboarding turns basic body metrics, activity level, goal and budget into a usable plan.

Use:
`Build a plan from your body metrics, goal, experience and routine.`

Avoid:
`Medical-grade recommendations`, `guaranteed transformation`, or `doctor-approved`.

### Daily Dashboard

The dashboard brings nutrition, workout, progress, achievements and profile into one place.

Use:
`A daily dashboard for training, meals, water, sleep and progress.`

### Nutrition + Habits

Nutrition is connected to the training day, with macro targets, calories, water and sleep.

Use:
`Macro targets, meal plans, calorie lookup, water and sleep live in one flow.`

### Workout + Phase Logic

Workout plans include splits, exercise details, rest timing, phase advisor and support guide.

Use:
`Train from a weekly split, then adjust with phase guidance as you progress.`

### Progress + Motivation

Progress tracking includes weight, body fat, measurements, weekly reports, badges, profile photos and share cards.

Use:
`Make consistency visible before motivation disappears.`

## One-Line Value Proposition

Full Balance gives you a free-forever wellness dashboard for workouts, meals, habits and progress.

## Elevator Pitch

Full Balance is a personal wellness web app for people who want structure without friction or subscription pressure. It creates a daily flow around your training plan, nutrition targets, water, sleep, body measurements, achievements and shareable progress, so your routine is easier to follow from your phone. It is completely free and will stay that way.

## Proof Points From Current App

- 5 main dashboard sections: Nutrition, Workout, Progress, Achievements, Profile.
- Nutrition plan with calorie/macro targets and food lookup.
- Workout panel with weekly split, exercise details, rest timer and program advisor.
- Progress tracking with weight, body fat, measurements and weekly summary.
- Achievements and share card for motivation.
- Profile progress photos and data export.
- PWA manifest and responsive/mobile-first UI.
- TR / EN / ES language support.
- No paywall, no subscription, no card requirement.

## Claim Guardrails

- Use `free forever`, `no card required`, `no paywall`, and `web app` for current launch.
- Use `personalized`, not `medical`.
- Use `AI-powered` only when referring to plan generation/adaptive logic at a product-marketing level; avoid implying clinical intelligence.
- Do not publish `Plus`, `Pro`, `premium`, `paid`, `subscription`, `trial`, `upgrade`, or `limited free plan` claims.
- Do not describe RevenueCat as a future monetization layer. If RevenueCat appears in planning, frame it only as `no paid offering / no paywall` documentation.
