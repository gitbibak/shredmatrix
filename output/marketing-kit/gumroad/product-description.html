Full Balance is a calm, mobile-first wellness dashboard for your daily routine: personal onboarding, workout planning, nutrition and macro targets, calorie lookup, water and sleep tracking, body measurements, weekly summaries, achievements, profile tools and share cards.

The core promise is simple: Full Balance is completely free and will stay free. No card required. No subscription. No premium wall.

What is included?

  • Personal plan flow based on your metrics and goal
  • Nutrition dashboard with daily calories and macro targets
  • Calorie lookup and food search
  • Weekly workout split with sets, reps and rest details
  • Rest timer, program advisor and phase guidance
  • Water and sleep tracking
  • Weight, body fat and body measurement records
  • Weekly summaries and progress charts
  • Achievement badges
  • Profile, progress photos and data export
  • Shareable Full Balance card
  • Mobile-first web app / PWA experience
  • English, Turkish and Spanish interface support

Who is it for?

  • People who want to start training but do not know where to begin
  • Users who want workouts and nutrition in the same place
  • People tracking signals like weight, body fat, measurements, water and sleep
  • Mobile-first users who want quick daily check-ins
  • Anyone who wants a wellness plan without subscription pressure

What is it not?

  • It is not medical advice.
  • It does not promise guaranteed transformation.
  • It does not replace a doctor, dietitian or trainer.
  • It is not a paid membership or premium package.
